The CIViC Variant Evidence Score allows curators to assess the accumulation of evidence for each variant. The CIViC Variant Evidence Score is calculated by adding all Evidence Item Scores for each variant. The Evidence Item Score is calculated by multiplying the evidence level (A=10 points, B=5 points, C=3 points, D=1 point, E=0.25 points) by the trust rating (Each Star = 1 point).
